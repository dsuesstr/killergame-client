5-in-a-row

Killergame is a variant of the famous game Gomoku. The only difference between Gomoku and Killergame is that the latter is played on a 10×10 field.

The rules

The rules are simple: Orange plays first, and players alternate in placing a stone of their color on an empty field. The winner is the first player to get an unbroken row of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

For every win you get 3 points and every draw gives you 1 point. A game ends draw if all the fields are occupied yet no player managed to get 5 stones in a row.

Instructions

After registering or logging in you are automatically sent to the Lobby where you have a list of Games and Available Players.

Available Players

This section lists all the currently logged in players. You can either check their stats (using the icon) or challenge them to a Killergame (using the icon).

Games

In this section you see challenges by other players which you can either accept (by using the icon) or decline (by using the icon).

Your own challenges of other players are also listed here (indicated by the icon). You can also cancle challenges (using the icon).

Accepting a challenge

After accepting a challenge by another player you will be sent to the Game. Since the challenger is always Orange (and therefore places the first stone) you have to wait until he or she makes a move.

Someone accepts your challenge

If a player you challenged has accepted, the Start Game icon appears which brings you directly to the game.

Playing

If it is your turn you can place one stone on any non occupied field. After doing so, you have to wait until your opponent has placed his stone.

You can forfeit a game anytime by clicking the red button with the icon.

Note that you have 30 seconds to complete your move. After those 30 seconds the system will automatically forfeit the game for you.

If one player manages to build an unbroken row of five stones (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally), the winning row will be highlighted and the game is over. Use the Home button to return to the lobby.

And now: Enjoy a Killergame!
